The Problem & Our Solution

❌ The Problem

  • Montgomery's city data is scattered across multiple portals
  • Residents can't easily query or understand 911/safety data
  • Media coverage skews public perception of safety
  • City officials lack tools to compare perception vs reality
  • 35% of 911 calls are non-emergency — wasting resources
→

✅ SafetyLens Solution

  • AI agent unifies 5 city datasets in natural language
  • Ask any question in plain English — get data-driven answers
  • Bright Data scrapes news to compare perception vs data
  • Interactive dashboards reveal actionable patterns
  • Recommends 311 diversion to reduce 911 overload by ~35%
